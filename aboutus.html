About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to help every person feel comfortable within their own bodies. By allowing members to choose different trainers based on their skills and ratings, we hope to give members a safe environment to learn a different skill or simply get in shape.

Why Wahoo Workouts?

There are many gyms and personal trainers out there. So why choose us? Well, we offer you the opportunity to meet a personalized trainer for you that aims to help you reach your goals. Through our custom training page, you can specify what the workout should be, how long you want to workout, and where to meet up. It’s that simple!

Choosing a trainer has never been easier! Simply scroll through our list of trainers, each of whom offer a different specialty, and select one that feels right for you. At the end of each workout, members can rate their trainers, ensuring that the quality of our trainers never diminishes.



Are you a personal trainer just starting out? Our system provides the perfect platform to grow your base of clients. Our system will connect you with individuals both passionate about improving themself and ready to dive into your preferred fitness area. We also promote the highest quality training. When you receive high ratings from your students, we reward you by featuring you on our home page to help you grow even more.
